About This Website

Hey! My name is Grace and I'm the creator of this website! I made this website over the summer of 2022 through a Girls Who Code program. I chose to make my website about LGBTQ equality because it's something that I relate to on a personal level. As someone who is LGBTQ, it is often difficult to find a safe community and be able to fully express myself. By bringing to light these issues, I hope that more people will be educated and understand the struggles that LGBTQ people go through everyday.
