Educate

Educating yourself is crucial to minimizing harmful misconceptions you may have. It also allows you to see these issues in a new light and better understand the struggles that LGBTQ people face everyday.


Here are some great resources to get you started!

The Asexual Visibility and Education Network (AVEN)
Bi.org - Frequent Questions About Bisexuality
Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) - LGBT Health
Human Rights Campaign - Pronouns
Human Rights Campaign - Myths about Transgender Rights
National Center for Transgender Equality - Understanding Transgender People
The Trevor Project - Resource Center
The Trevor Project - Gay and Lesbian Identities
