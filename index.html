LGBTQ+ Rights and Equality

The LGBTQ+ community has experienced discrimination and violence for decades for having a gender and sexuality that differs from society’s norms, and it is our job to take back the rights that were stripped away from us and ensure that all LGBTQ+ people feel safe and protected.

Statistics from 2022

According to Trevor Project’s 2022 National Survey on LGBTQ Youth Mental Health (May 2022)

42%
of LGBTQ youth seriously attempted committing suicide

75%
of LGBTQ youth have experienced discrimination based on their sexual orientation or gender identity at least once in their lifetime

80+%
of LGBTQ youth found their living situation more stressful because of COVID

72%
of LGBTQ youth had symptoms of generalized anxiety disorder in the past two weeks

Only
1/3
of LGBTQ youth lived in a supportive household

62%
of LGBTQ youth had symptoms of major depressive disorder in the past two weeks
